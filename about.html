Brand Story

Born from the hush before a bird returns to the glove.

Aerie Encounters began as a small education program for local schools and grew into a premium wildlife experience without abandoning its core promise: every guest should leave with more respect than spectacle.

Our site is built around bookings, programs, gift certificates, and storytelling. It does not include an admin dashboard.

Falconry Heritage

Ancient practice, contemporary responsibility.

Falconry is one of humanity’s oldest relationships with birds of prey. We teach that history alongside modern science, welfare standards, telemetry, and habitat conservation.

Wildlife Ethics

A premium experience should never cost the bird its ease.

We limit group sizes, shorten handling time when needed, avoid forced contact, and change plans when a bird, handler, or weather condition asks for a slower day.
